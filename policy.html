KH&CO – Our Policy

Commission & Payment

As every project at KH&CO is a bespoke commission tailored to specific dimensions and timber preferences, we adhere to the following payment structure:

  • Deposit: A non-refundable 60% deposit is required to secure your slot in our production schedule and to procure raw materials.
  • Balance: The remaining 40% is payable upon completion, prior to or on the day of delivery/installation.
  • Quotes: Our quotes remain valid for 14 days due to the fluctuating costs of premium timber and materials.

Lead Times & Craftsmanship

Mastery cannot be rushed. Our standard lead times range from 14 to 21 business days, depending on the complexity of the commission and current workshop volume.

  • Handcrafted Nature: Minor variations in wood grain, color, and texture are natural characteristics of solid timber and are not considered defects.
  • Design Changes: Any changes made to the design after the deposit has been paid may incur additional costs and extend the lead time.

Delivery & Installation

We serve the Gauteng region (Johannesburg & Pretoria). Delivery fees are calculated based on the distance from our workshop and the scale of the installation.

  • Site Access: Clients must ensure that the installation area is cleared and accessible for our team.
  • Collection: Small furniture pieces may be collected from our workshop by prior appointment.

By proceeding with a deposit, you acknowledge and agree to the KH&CO terms of service. For specific inquiries regarding our warranty on joinery, please contact us directly.
