The KH&CO Legacy

Based in the heart of Gauteng, KH&CO (a proud division of ThokeGroup) represents the pinnacle of bespoke timber engineering. While the brand itself has been a distinguished name in the industry since 2016, our workshop is powered by decades of inherited woodworking wisdom.

We believe that carpentry is more than just assembly—it is a dialogue between the craftsman and the material. Whether we are designing a minimalist kitchen island or a commercial-grade mobile food cart, our approach remains the same: Precision, Durability, and Soul.
